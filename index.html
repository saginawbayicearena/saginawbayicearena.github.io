Greater Saginaw Amateur Hockey Association

Welcome to The Greater Saginaw Amateur Hockey Association. Our goal is to promote, govern, regulate and improve organized hockey under its jurisdiction. To foster among its member, supporters, and teams, a general community spirit. To maintain and increase the interest in the game of hockey. To exercise supervision and direction over the playing interest of its players, coaches, managers, referees, sponsors, and executives, with emphasis on the enhancement of good character, sportsmanship and citizenship.
